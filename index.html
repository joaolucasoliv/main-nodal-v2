What is NODAL

We activate trusted networks of urban changemakers: governments, communities, institutions and independent experts, using community, data and AI to make projects work on the ground.

NODAL is the infrastructure layer connecting capital, talent, knowledge and implementation in urban development.

Not just a platform. A system for activation, execution and coordination. Starting in Latin America, scaling globally.

The problem

Urban projects keep failing. Not for lack of money, but for lack of coordination.

01

Fragmented ecosystems

Actors don't connect at scale: weak engagement, no trusted local networks.

02

Poor coordination

Limited access to knowledge, opportunities and collaborators.

03

Wasted talent & capital

Delays, social conflict, idle capital. Burnout and underused talent.

No system connects actors at scale while supporting the people doing the work.

Market insight

A structural imbalance between urban talent supply and system needs.

20–50architects for every transport engineer graduating each year
1.5–2.5Mcivil engineers across the region, yet mobility talent is scarce
1–3Kmobility engineers graduate annually, in 22 countries
$B+projects from millions to billions exposed to social risk

Environmental engineers, sociologists, communicators, civic leaders: highly distributed, deeply disconnected. NODAL connects them.

The core idea

A matching platform built on the logic of networks.

Every person and organisation is a node; every trusted relationship, an edge. Together they form the graph of Latin American urbanism. NODAL finds the connections that make projects happen.
