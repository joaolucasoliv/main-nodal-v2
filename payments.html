FAQ

Billing questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly plans stop at the end of the paid month; annual plans at the end of the paid year. Your free Member account stays active either way.

What currency will I be charged in?

Prices are set in US dollars. Local payment methods (Mercado Pago, Yape, Plin) will charge the equivalent in your local currency at checkout.

Will I get a receipt?

Every charge comes with an emailed receipt, and invoices for organisations will be available on request.

Is this pricing final?

No — this is founding-member preview pricing. Whatever you see at launch is what early members keep.
