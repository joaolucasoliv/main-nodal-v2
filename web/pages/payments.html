FAQ

Membership questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly plans stop at the end of the paid month; annual plans at the end of the paid year. Your free community account stays active either way.

What currency will I be charged in?

The checkout provider shows the final charge before payment.

Will I get a receipt?

Every charge comes with an emailed receipt, and organization invoices are available on request.

Is this pricing final?

No. This is founding-member pricing. Early supporters will keep the launch price they choose.

How will payments be processed?

Through a certified checkout provider. You pay on their secure page and come straight back; NODAL never sees or stores card numbers.
